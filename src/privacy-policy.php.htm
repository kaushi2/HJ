What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small amount of data that is placed in the browser of your computer or on your mobile device. So-called "first-party cookies" are cookies served by the entity operating the domain through which the cookie is served. HalalJourney.com's own cookies are therefore "first-party cookies". If we allow others to use cookies through HalalJourney.com websites and apps, these cookies are called "third-party cookies."

There is also a difference between session cookies and permanent cookies. Session cookies only exist while you are using your browser. Permanent cookies have a longer lifespan and are not terminated when you close your browser. We strive to serve cookies or allow the serving of cookies with a maximum lifespan of 5 years. In exceptional circumstances, like for security purposes and where absolutely necessary, our cookies may have a longer lifespan than that.

Other tracking technologies are used which are similar to cookies. These can include web beacons (also known as pixel tags, web bugs, or gifs), tracking URLs, or software development kits (SDKs). A web beacon is a tiny graphic image of just one pixel that can be delivered to your computer as part of a web page request in an app, in an advertisement, or in an HTML email message. Pixels can be used to retrieve info from your device, like your device type or operating system, internet provider address, and time of your visit. They are also used to serve and read cookies in your browser. Tracking URLs are used to understand from what referring website the HalalJourney.com websites or apps are used. SDKs are small pieces of code included in apps that function a bit like cookies and web beacons.

These technologies taken together are referred to in this Cookie Statement as "cookies".
How are cookies used?

Cookies are used for a variety of different purposes. They allow you to be recognised as the same user across the different pages of a website, between websites, or when you use an app.

Our website and apps use cookies for different purposes:

Technical Cookies: we try to give our visitors an advanced, user-friendly website and apps that adapts automatically to their needs and wishes. To achieve this we use technical cookies to show you our website, to make it function correctly, to create your user account, to sign you in, and to manage your bookings. These technical cookies are necessary for our website to function properly.

Functional Cookies: we also use functional cookies to remember your preferences and assist you to use our website and apps efficiently and effectively. For example, these cookies remember your preferred currency, language, your searches, and your previously viewed accommodations. We may also use cookies to remember your registration info so you do not have to keep retyping your login credentials every time you visit our site. Your password will, however, always be encrypted for greater security. These functional cookies are not absolutely necessary for our website to work, but they make it easier to use and enhance your HalalJourney.com experience.
